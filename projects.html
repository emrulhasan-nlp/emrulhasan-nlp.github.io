Postdoc project at UBC & BC Cancer. Developing an agentic conversational assistant for personalised cancer resource navigation and clinical decision support. Research focus on evaluation frameworks for safety, faithfulness, and reliability in patient-facing healthcare AI.

Responsible AI · Multimodal
Multimodal LLM for News Media Bias Detection
Created the NewsMediaBias-Plus dataset for analysing media bias and disinformation in news articles with associated visual content. Applied LLMs (Llama, Mistral) and multimodal models (CLIP, LLaVA, MiniCPM, PaliGemma, Phi3 Vision) to establish scalable benchmarks for responsible AI evaluation.

MRRRec: Multi-Criteria Rating & Review Recommendation
Deep neural network integrating multicriteria ratings with textual review features via attention mechanisms. Achieves state-of-the-art performance across multiple benchmark datasets. Published at IEEE Big Data.

Recommendation · NLP · BERT
Aspect-Aware Multi-Criteria Recommendation with BERT
Fine-tuned BERT for criteria rating prediction with an aspect representation layer, enabling explainable multi-criteria recommendation. Evaluated on Tripadvisor, RateBeer, and BeerAdvocate. Demonstrated superior performance over prior baselines.

Recommendation · Clustering
Multi-Criteria Recommendation via Semantic Clustering
Uses semantic similarity-based sentence clustering and sentiment analysis to discover implicit and undefined rating criteria from user reviews. Outperforms baselines on TripAdvisor and BeerAdvocate. Published at ASONAM 2024.

Recommendation · Tourism · RecSys
Accommodation Review Ranking for Tourism
2nd place at the RecTour Challenge (RecSys 2024, Bari). Built user/item profile encoding with sentence transformers for feature extraction to rank accommodation reviews for tourism recommendation.
