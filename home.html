Hello! I'm Emrul Hasan, a recent PhD in Computer Science specializing in recommendation systems and NLP, with over five years of hands-on experience in Python and PyTorch. My research interests lie at the intersection of recommendation systems, generative AI, agentic AI, and responsible AI, with a focus on robustness, trust, and reliability.

I currently work as a GenAI and Agentic AI Technical Specialist at the Vector Institute, focusing on prototyping and implementing proof-of-concept (PoC) systems. Previously, I worked at Amazon as an Applied Scientist Intern, where I contributed to the development of evaluation methods for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems.

I have over five years of teaching experience across undergraduate and graduate courses in computer science, physics, and business. My research has appeared in leading venues, including ACM Computing Surveys. I am passionate about AI innovation, with a focus on robustness and the responsible use of AI in high-stakes domains.
